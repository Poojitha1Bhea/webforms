Studying English with ELS will allow you to visit many exciting global destinations. We offer you the ability to first master your English language skills and then study at universities in countries like the United States or Canada. Many students from around the world choose to begin their English studies in cities and countries that have unique educational and cultural opportunities.


Our Pathway, through the focal point of Latin American diversity that is Panama City, will give you the experience of learning English at a significantly lower price than the US, while being immersed in a Central American culture unlike any other.


The culture of Panama City contains Spanish, American, Afro-Caribbean and Indian influences.


This thriving commercial city combines historic colonial architecture with many modern buildings and has much to offer in terms of entertainment both during the day and at night.


ELS plans a wide variety of activities for students, from guided walks to local attractions, sporting events, visits to nearby theaters, museums and restaurants, American conversation partners, and of course excursions to shopping malls, beaches, and parks Natural.


With its unique position on world trade routes, this thriving commercial city attracts immigrants from all over the world, and welcomes you for a lifetime experience of studying English like no other!


Program: English for Academic Purposes - 30 lessons.


Levels 101-109 are available in Panama; Students must complete Levels 110 through 112 in Canada or the United States.
