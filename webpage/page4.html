ACADEMIC ENGLISH PROGRAMS (AE2, AE3, AE4)

International students will study English and prepare for success in Australian higher education with Academic English AE2, AE3, and AE4. The fast-paced, challenging coursework of ELS Australia's Academic English programs prepare you for direct entry into higher education in Australia - AE2 provides foundation skills for academic study, AE3 prepares you to study at a TAFE or vocational college,and AE4 prepares you for university study.

GENERAL ENGLISH PROGRAM

ELS Australia's General English programs support you as you improve essential English language skills. The General English program in Sydney, available in both the day and the evening, can increase your confidence in using English during everyday business and social situations. Practice your conversation skills, improve your writing and grammar, and develop strategic listening skills. If higher education is your goal, after a minimum of four weeks in the General English program, you can take the Academic English entry test.
