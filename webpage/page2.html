ABOUT THE ELS EAP PROGRAM

Through the EAP program, you will learn listening, speaking, reading, writing, pronunciation, and grammar. You will also get the skills you need to be successful in a university setting abroad, including:


  • Critical Thinking
  • Presentations/public speaking
  • Note Taking
  • Public Research

Our morning classes consist of Structure and Speaking Practice or Language Studies, Vocabulary Enrichment (Levels 101-109) OR Skills Enhancement Classes (Master's only), and one lesson in our Language Technology Center (LTC). Our afternoon classes are dedicated to reading and writing.

Successfully completing Level 112 of the ELS EAP program earns you a Certificate of Completion accepted as Proof of English proficiency at more than 650 colleges and universities worldwide. Many two-year schools will accept Level 109.
